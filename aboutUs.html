About Us

Inspiring eco-heroes through play, learning, and action

With EcoQuest,environmental education becomes an engaging adventure where every mission creates real-world impact.

Our Vision

Our platform turns environmental learning into a game, a story, and a mission.

Students explore topics like waste segregation, water conservation, biodiversity, and air pollution through storylines and levels. Each topic has quizzes, mini-games, and projects. Students can upload real-life sustainability projects—like tree plantation or waste segregation at home—and the most-liked projects rise to the top to inspire others. Completing missions earns eco-points, badges, and leaderboard positions. Eco-points aren't just virtual—they can be redeemed for real-life sustainable products like cloth bags, reusable bottles, or even tree sapling kits.
